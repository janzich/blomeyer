Personal Training

Sessions for members

We offer a “Personal Training Request Form” for members that are interested in the service at the front desk. Please fill out a request form or call 404-727-4600 for more information.

Personal training should be preceded by a complimentary fitness consultation with our certified and degreed personal trainers. At the conclusion of your fitness consultation, the trainer will answer any questions that you have in regards to personal training and how it can help you achieve your fitness goals.

To schedule personal training sessions, please stop in at the front desk or call 404-727-4600.
