Group Fitness Classes

Note: All of our group fitness classes will benefit your health in all of the listed categories!

Yoga

  • Categories: Stress Management, Strength
  • The practice of yoga uses a variety of postures (asanas) and breathing exercises (pranyam) to improve posture, balance, and coordination. Regular practice gradually increases strength, flexibility and overall health of muscles, organs, joints and spine. A great stress relief!

Rhythmic Step / Step'n'Sculpt

  • Categories: Strength, Weight Management
  • A cardiovascular workout guaranteed to burn fat and build/tone muscles with basic cardio and weight lifting routine combinations using the step. A fun, high energy body & mind aerobics class structured to give a full body work-out!

Boost & Burn

  • Categories: Strength, Weight Management
  • An exciting class that focuses on upper and lower body strengthening and toning. Class concludes with a great ab work-out!

The Cyclone

  • Categories: Weight Management, Blood Pressure, Cholesterol
  • An aerobics class for every age and fitness level! This cycling workout simulates flat and hill training and utilizes a range of resistance and body cycling positions. Class size limited to the number of bikes available.

Cardio Sculpt

  • Categories: Strength, Weight Management, Blood Pressure
  • A power-packed hour of intense workout moves designed to improve cardiovascular fitness. This class is simple to follow and easily modified for any fitness level.

Boot Camp / Body Blast

  • Categories: Strength
  • Calling all men and women! Conditioning class consisting of jumping rope, running, kick boxing, or callisthenic cardio activities with alternating segments of weight training while utilizing weights or body bars - Are you up for the challenge?

Just move it!

  • Categories: Blood Pressure, Cholesterol
  • An aerobic workout that includes light weights for upper body strength and conditioning, low impact movement for cardiovascular conditioning, and a strong component of floor work for flexibility and muscle toning.

Pilates

  • Categories: Stress Management, Strength
  • Connect your mind and body through the methods of Joseph Pilates. This graceful workout builds abdominal and torso strength while improving posture, flexibility, and balance. Exercises are coordinated with breathing and deep abdominal control. (Mats are provided, but bring your own if youâ€™ve got it!)

Pump'n'Run

  • Categories: Strength, Weight Management
  • A fun and challenging combination of cardio and resistance training upstairs on the track. A great way to start the weekend!

Express Sculpt

  • Categories: Strength
  • Upper and lower body strengthening/toning/conditioning with short bursts of high intensity interval training.

Rip'n'Ride

  • Categories: Strength, Weight Management, Blood Pressure
  • Get your cardio and strength training all in one work-out! Burn calories and increase lean muscle mass to burn even more calories after your workout.

Zumba

  • Categories: Weight Management, Blood Pressure, Cholesterol
  • Party yourself into shape with this Latin-inspired, calorie burning dance aerobics class. A guaranteed fun work-out for every fitness level!

Track with Shana

  • Categories: Strength, Weight Management, Blood Pressure, Cholesterol
  • Walk/jog class for all fitness levels on the indoor track that focuses on mileage and muscular strength.

Double Duty with Beth and Greg

  • Categories: Strength
  • 30 minute intense, interval class that incorporates upper and lower body strength training by using ropes!

Butts & Guts with Jessie

  • Categories: Strength
  • 30 minute class that focuses on abs and glutes. Come if you dare!

Powercore with Cody

  • Categories: Strength
  • 30 minute class that focuses on abs and upper body, using the Reebok Core Boards to optimize core engagement!
