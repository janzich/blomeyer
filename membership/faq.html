Frequently Asked Questions

Who runs this fitness center?

  • The Blomeyer Health Fitness center is managed by Corporate Sports Unlimited. Donald (Don) H. Whitney is the president of Corporate Sports Unlimited. Shana Ricker is the General Manager of the Blomeyer Health Fitness Center.

How long can I stay in the parking deck?

  • Your parking hang tag allows you to stay in the parking deck for 2 hours only.

When are your aerobic classes?

  • Class format and times may vary from month to month, so please check our web site for the current schedule of classes. Classes are free for all Blomeyer Health Fitness Center at Emory members.

How big is your aerobic studio?

  • The aerobics studio is approximately 3000 square feet.

How much are fitness assessments for non-members?

  • $45
