General Guidelines

Amenities and operating procedures

We are pleased to provide you with information about the Center’s amenities and operating procedures. We encourage you to let us know at any time if you have concerns about the Center and your membership privileges and responsibilities. Be assured that each staff member of the Earle B. and Stephanie S. Blomeyer Health Fitness Center is dedicated to making each of your visits healthy and enjoyable.

The Center will be closed on all official Emory University holidays: New Year’s Day, Martin Luther King Jr.’s Birthday, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, the Day after Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, and Christmas Day. See list of holidays and closing dates under the About Us tab.
