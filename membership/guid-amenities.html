Amenities

Enjoy The Atlanta Journal-Constitution or one of our magazines while you relax in our lounge area or while exercising on one of our cardiovascular machines.

The Blomeyer Health Fitness Center stocks a variety of nutrition bars and refreshing beverages to quench your thirst and provide you with quick energy!

As an added service to our members, we will offer a Resource Library of books and videos on a variety of health and fitness related subjects. Please see the attendant at our front desk to check out your selection.

For your convenience, the Center has a selection of tee shirts, shorts, tank tops, and socks available for purchase.

The Center has four closed-circuit televisions in our workout area. If you wish to tune into one of these stations while you are on one of our machines, please bring your portable player and headphones.

Our elegant locker rooms are supplied for your convenience with body wash, shampoo, conditioner, deodorant, lotion, shave cream, mouthwash, and hair spray. We also provide hair dryers, curling irons and electric curlers in our locker rooms for your complete care and comfort. Our front desk staff can assist you with an iron, ironing board, or steamer if you have a need for these items as well.
