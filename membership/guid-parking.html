Parking

Parking remains an increasingly precious commodity along the Clifton corridor. To foster community and environmental health as well as your personal fitness, the Center encourages its members to walk or to use alternative transportation whenever possible when visiting the Center.

However, if you must drive, The Blomeyer Health Fitness Center, in concert with the Emory Office of Community Services, has arranged limited parking for Fitness Center members on the top level of the deck adjacent to the 1525 Clifton Road Building. An Emory hangtag activated for the Blomeyer Center is needed for access to the top level of the parking deck. Bring your hangtag to the Center’s front desk on your first visit so that we may activate it for deck access. If you have a current, official Emory Parking hang tag, you may park on the top level of the 1525 Building deck for a maximum of two hours at no charge while you are using the Blomeyer Health Fitness Center.

If you do not have an Emory Parking hang tag, you have these options:

  • If you are an Emory employee, you must obtain a parking tag from Emory parking services. Please call 404-727-7275 (Emory parking services) for more information.
  • If you are not an Emory employee, you may purchase a hangtag through our front desk for an additional $15 per month. If you currently have a monthly bank draft from your checking account, we will simply add the $15 to the amount you are currently being deducted, payable until you cancel your membership. This hangtag will allow you up to two hours’ parking at any time you are working out in The BHFC.
  • You may choose to time your workouts in the Center so that you leave the parking deck before 8:00 am or come in after 5:00pm Monday through Friday when the parking is free. Likewise, parking is free on weekends.
  • You may choose to pay the hourly rate to the attendant for parking when necessary.
