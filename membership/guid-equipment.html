Polar Body Age Fitness Assessment and Equipment Orientations

Upon joining the fitness center, we will have you fill out a health history questionnaire. After your health history questionnaire has been reviewed, you will be eligible for two complimentary sessions:

  • Polar Body Age Fitness Assessment
  • Equipment Orientation

Our comprehensive fitness assessment will give you an overall analysis of your current fitness level. One of our Wellness Associates will conduct the assessment and interpret the results to determine your current fitness level. The assessment includes height, weight, body composition, upper/lower body strength, flexibility, estimation of your aerobic capacity and nutritional guidelines. Once you have completed the fitness assessment, your next step is the Equipment Orientation.

This is done by one of our Wellness Associates who will provide a tour and explanation of equipment. You will be oriented to the proper use of our Cybex VR2 and cardiovascular equipment. A workout card will be provided so that you can better understand and utilize the information given in your future workouts. If at the end of your equipment orientation you would like further information about personal instruction, the Wellness Associate will review personal training packages, which we offer.

We look forward to you scheduling an appointment for your personal health and equipment orientation with one of our trained professionals. You may schedule your personal Polar Body Age Fitness Assessment or Equipment Orientation by calling The Blomeyer Health Fitness Center at (404) 727-4600. Please wear comfortable exercise clothing for your fitness assessment.
