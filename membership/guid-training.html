Training and Massage Therapy

Personal training

We offer personal training services in our facility for an additional fee. Our Corporate Sports personal trainers have degrees in Exercise Science and are nationally certified. Your initial consultation is free!

Massage therapy

Massage therapy is available on-site by appointment only. Please call the front desk at 404-727-4600 to schedule an appointment. (Contact front desk or refer to the Services section of our website for massage menu and pricing.)

Classes

We offer a wide variety of classes to accommodate all fitness levels. Some of the types of classes we offer are Yoga, Step, Just Move It, Sculpt, Boot Camp, Pilates and Spinning classes. Our instructors are knowledgeable and certified, and there is no additional charge to attend these classes. The current class schedule is available in the Group Fitness section of our website.

Equipment

We feature a complete line of Cybex strength training equipment, as well as free weights for specific conditioning. Our state-of-the-art Gravitron machine is the latest tool in upper body strength training. Our cardiovascular area includes Stairmasters, upright and recumbent Lifecycles, Concept II rowers, treadmills, Life Fitness cross-trainers, and Precor elliptical Transports. Or, if you prefer to walk or run in a controlled-temperature environment, you will enjoy our 1/16-mile indoor track on the mezzanine level.

Special events and programs

We like to make exercise fun! We will be offering special events from time to time that will help you put more activity in your life. Our monthly newsletter will keep you informed of all of our special events and programs.

Each of us at the Blomeyer Health Fitness Center is excited about being your partner in health and fitness. We look forward to working with you and to providing the best in professional services so that you may reach your fitness goals.
