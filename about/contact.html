Directions

Parking for BHFC members is available in the parking deck adjacent to the back of the 1525 building. Please park on the 5th floor.

From Interstate 20 - Take exit 60-B, the Moreland Avenue North exit. Turn right on Moreland and follow it approximately 3 miles. Moreland will change to Briarcliff Road once it crosses Ponce de Leon Avenue. Continue straight on Briarcliff approximately 2 miles to North Decatur Road. Turn right and follow North Decatur one and half mile to the Clifton Road intersection and turn left on Clifton. Continue about one mile straight; the 1525 building will be on your right. Follow signs for parking.

From Interstate 75 - Take exit 248-C, the Freedom Parkway exit. Cross Boulevard; continue on Freedom Parkway; veer left at split; continue until it ends at Ponce de Leon Avenue; then turn right. Off Ponce, turn left on Briarcliff Road. Go approximately 2 miles to North Decatur Road. Turn right and follow North Decatur one and half mile to the Clifton Road intersection and turn left on Clifton. Continue about one mile straight; the 1525 building will be on your right. Follow signs for parking.

From Interstate 85 North - Take exit 248-C, the Freedom Parkway exit. Cross Boulevard; continue on Freedom Parkway; veer left at split; continue until it ends at Ponce de Leon Avenue; then turn right. Off Ponce, turn left on Briarcliff Road. Go approximately 2 miles to North Decatur Road. Turn right and follow North Decatur one and half mile to the Clifton Road intersection and turn left on Clifton. Continue about one mile straight; the 1525 building will be on your right. Follow signs for parking.

From Interstate 85 South - Take exit 91, the Clairmont Road exit. Turn left (east) on Clairmont and follow it approximately one and half mile to Mason Mill Road. Follow Mason Mill and turn left on Houston Mill Road. After passing the Harwood apartment complex on your left, turn left on North Gatewood Road. The entrance to the 1525 building parking deck will be immediately on your right.

MARTA - Bus number 6 stop directly in front of the 1525 building. Check the MARTA schedule.
