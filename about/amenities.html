Features and Amenities

Features

Occupying 14,000 square feet on the top floor of Emory’s 1525 Clifton Building, the Blomeyer Health Fitness Center offers a variety of health-enhancing options as well as panoramic views of the Emory campus.

Professionally staffed, easily accessible, reasonably priced, and offering the latest equipment and amenities, The Blomeyer Health Fitness Center at Emory takes your health and well-being to new heights.

Amenities

  • Cybex strength training equipment
  • Free weights for specific conditioning
  • Stairmasters, LifeFitness treadmills, LifeFitness crosstrainers, upright and recumbent Lifecycles, Concept II rowers, Gravitron, Precor Transports
  • Suspended indoor mezzanine track
  • Group fitness studio with video library
  • Men’s locker room facilities with sauna and TV
  • Women’s locker room facilities with sauna and TV
  • Complimentary towel service
  • Juice/health bar
  • Pro-Shop
